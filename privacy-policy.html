Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the TikTok Posting App ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use our service. This App is a personal automation tool used solely by its owner to upload and publish video content to TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share personal information from any third-party users. The only data processed by the App is:

  • Video files uploaded by the app owner from their own Google Drive
  • TikTok account credentials (OAuth tokens) used solely for posting content on behalf of the app owner

2. How We Use Information

Information processed by the App is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with the TikTok API on behalf of the app owner
  • Upload and publish video content to the app owner's TikTok account
  • Log content metadata (titles, captions, timestamps) to a private Google Sheet owned by the app owner

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any data with third parties. No user data is transmitted to any external service beyond what is required to authenticate with and post to TikTok via their official API.

4. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the following third-party services:

  • TikTok API – for publishing video content. Subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Drive / Google Sheets – for file storage and logging. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • OpenAI API – for generating captions and metadata. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

The App does not retain any personal data. OAuth tokens are stored securely in the automation platform and are used only during the posting process. No video content is stored by the App beyond what already exists in the app owner's Google Drive.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach out via the contact information associated with the TikTok Developer App registration.
